# 🇵🇸 Boycott for Gaza - Multimedia Campaign

## 📌 Project Overview
This multimedia project is a solidarity campaign designed to raise awareness about the ongoing situation in Palestine. It combines audio storytelling with a strong visual identity to deliver a clear message: **"Boycott for Gaza"** (قاطع علشان غزة).

## 🎧 1. Audio Production 
**Tool Used:** Audacity  
**File in:** `Audio_Production`

The audio track uses emotional contrast to deliver its message. It begins by describing a state of normal, peaceful life, which is suddenly disrupted by the unexpected reality of war and bombings. 
The narrative builds up to a powerful conclusion: *Even if there are superficial truces, the war and suffering have not truly ended, making the boycott a continuous necessity.*

## 🎨 2. Visual Identity 
**Tool Used:** Adobe Illustrator  
**Files in:** `Visual_Identity`

The logo is designed to visually translate the audio's narrative into a striking badge.
* **The Palestinian Flag & Map:** The map of historical Palestine is draped in the traditional Keffiyeh pattern, overlapping a circular Palestinian flag. This represents resilience and deep-rooted identity.
* **The Red Drops:** Dripping from the red triangle of the flag, symbolizing the ongoing sacrifices and the human cost of the war mentioned in the audio.
* **Typography:** The call to action "قاطع علشان غزة" (Boycott for Gaza) is placed prominently at the bottom with a bold, shadowed Arabic font to ensure immediate readability and impact.

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
* **Audio Engineering:** Audacity (Cutting, mixing, and sound effects)
* **Graphic Design:** Adobe Illustrator (Vector mapping, typography, and visual composition)

## 🎯 Conclusion
This project demonstrates how integrating audio engineering with graphic design can create a cohesive and impactful multimedia campaign. The transition from auditory storytelling to visual symbolism successfully ingrains the campaign's core message into the audience's mind.
